My Approach

The 4C Framework

Developed from five years of stakeholder work across banking and fintech; my proprietary alignment framework that moves projects from ambiguity to committed action.

01
Clarity
Every engagement begins by surfacing unstated assumptions and aligning all parties on the actual problem being solved; not the presenting symptom.
02
Conflict
Productive disagreement is data. I create structured space for competing perspectives to surface early, where resolution is cheap and learning is high.
03
Consensus
Through facilitated workshops and requirement validation sessions, I drive stakeholders from divergence to a documented, shared understanding of scope and priority.
04
Commitment
Consensus without accountability is wishful thinking. I close every engagement with RACI-mapped ownership, sign-off checkpoints, and measurable acceptance criteria.
