Project Case Studies

Seven engagements across banking, fintech, and government payments; each documented from problem statement through to measurable outcome.

Access Bank PLC
GL Reconciliation Automation
⚙️
Challenge

The end-of-day general ledger reconciliation process consumed 8+ hours of analyst time per cycle, was prone to manual keying errors, and delayed overnight reporting to senior management.

My Role

Led end-to-end requirements gathering, process mapping, UAT coordination, and RPA bot specification. Facilitated sessions with Finance, IT, and Audit stakeholders to align on exception-handling rules before development commenced.

Outcome
8 hrs → 45 min cycle time  |  89% reduction  |  Near-zero manual errors
RPA GL Reconciliation Process Mapping Banking Operations
